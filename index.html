Who I Am

Hey there! I’m Hayleigh, your local Treasure Valley photographer, and owner of Wood Works Photography.
My love for photography started my sophomore year in high school. I started out small, entering hometown art contests, photography challenges, and learning from one of the greatest teachers to ever live. I quickly realized this was something I wanted to do long term, because creating beautiful art and preserving memories for others is one of the most amazing gifts you can possibly give someone. I hope we can connect soon, and start making some memories!
